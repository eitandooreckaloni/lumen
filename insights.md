some thoughts or insights about this or coaching

prospecting 
- who has a career i'd be interested in modeling after?
- who do i see with similar path to me? same school, same city, same field of interest, etc.? 

reaching out / the linkedin message
- reaching out shouldnt be about you wanting to work at their company. people dont like feeling used. its ALL ABOUT THEM, and what you think THEY bring to the table with all their experience
- people love feeling special and feelng valued. we reach out to them, make them feel like kings/queens. 

the call
- in the call, i want to turn them into a mentor/ally. how do i get them on my side, to think WITH ME how WE get me this job? 
- top priority to get intros to their friends. "who's 1 person u can think of that's relevant for me to talk to based on our chat about XYZ?"
- interview prep - what do u guys look for? how do u evaluate? how can i invest my time best at home?
